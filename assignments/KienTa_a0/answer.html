A good visualization example




I like this visualization because government's budget has been a huge vast area that only interative and attractive visualization can help people understand it. And this map is exactly the visualization we are looking for. The color help we visually infer how government spends their budget against different categories, and also to compare between different categories - for example medicare vs social security. Moreover, the categories are grouped in different big groups (Health Care, National Defense, etc) - where each group have categories with similar color (ex: National Defense has color yellow, Income Security has color green) that helps us in comparing categories of the same group or categories in different groups.

A bad visualization example




This is a bad visualization because the origin of the y-axis is not starting at 0, which make the visua lization misleading. In fact, the diiferent in subscription of the age 45-54 (lowest - 82%) is not that different from that of the age 18-24 (highest - 98%). However, the misleading visualization starting at 80% make us misunderstand that there is a huge gap between the lowest and the higest. Moreover, the visualization lacks the label of x_axis and y_axis to inform the information to viewer.
